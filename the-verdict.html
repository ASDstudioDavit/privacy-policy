THE VERDICT - Privacy Policy

ASDstudio · Online Multiplayer · Epic Games Store · Steam · Xbox · PlayStation
Effective date: July 4, 2026

ASDstudio does not sell personal data. THE VERDICT is an online multiplayer game and may use Epic Online Services and platform services to provide login, lobbies, matchmaking, peer-to-peer networking, friends, invites, presence, achievements, and platform features.

Information ASDstudio Collects Directly

ASDstudio does not directly collect your name, address, payment card information, contacts, photos, or precise location through THE VERDICT.

If you contact us for support, we may receive the information you choose to provide, such as your email address, message content, crash description, screenshots, or other support details. We use this information only to respond to your request and support the game.

Online Services

THE VERDICT may use platform and online services, including Epic Online Services, Epic Games Store, Steam, Xbox, PlayStation, or other platform services, for features such as:

  • account login and identity;
  • multiplayer lobbies and matchmaking;
  • peer-to-peer or online network connectivity;
  • friends, invites, and presence;
  • achievements, stats, or progression if enabled;
  • platform overlay features;
  • crash reporting, security, and platform compliance.

These services may process account identifiers, display names, network information, device information, session information, and other data required to provide online functionality. Such processing is governed by the privacy policies of the platform or service provider.

Epic Games Privacy Policy: https://legal.epicgames.com/epicgames/privacy-policy

Text Chat and Voice Chat

THE VERDICT may include text chat and voice chat features for online gameplay.

ASDstudio does not sell chat data. In the current implementation, ASDstudio does not intentionally store voice recordings or text chat logs on its own servers. Chat and voice features may be transmitted through platform or online service providers to deliver real-time gameplay communication.

Players should not share sensitive personal information in chat or voice communication.

Payments and Store Purchases

Purchases, refunds, entitlements, parental controls, and payment processing are handled by the store or platform where you access the game. ASDstudio does not receive your full payment card information.

Children's Privacy

THE VERDICT is intended for mature audiences. ASDstudio does not knowingly collect personal data from children. Platform-level age gates, parental controls, and child account protections may be handled by the platform provider.

Data Sharing

ASDstudio does not sell personal data. We may share information only when necessary to provide and maintain the game, respond to support requests, comply with legal obligations, protect the safety and integrity of the game and players, or work with platform providers and service providers that operate online features.

Data Retention

Support emails and related correspondence may be retained only as long as needed to resolve the request, maintain business records, or comply with legal obligations.

Game account, platform, purchase, matchmaking, and network data may be retained by the relevant platform or online service according to that provider's own privacy policy.

Your Choices

You may contact ASDstudio to ask privacy-related questions or request deletion of information you directly provided to us.

For Epic account data, store purchases, parental controls, or platform account information, please use the privacy tools and support channels provided by Epic Games or the relevant platform.

Contact

For any questions about privacy, reach us at davit@asdstudiogame.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this page as THE VERDICT, online features, platform requirements, or legal requirements evolve. Changes become effective upon posting here. The current version will always be available at this URL.
