Fire and Ashes Privacy Policy

Publisher: ASDstudio | Application: Fire and Ashes | Horror / Survival game
Effective date: October 2, 2025

ASDstudio does not sell personal data. Fire and Ashes is designed for offline core gameplay. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected, how it is used, how it is shared, how long it is retained, and how to contact ASDstudio for privacy or data protection inquiries.

Information ASDstudio Does Not Collect Through Core Gameplay

ASDstudio does not directly collect your real name, email address, postal address, payment card number, contacts, photos, precise location, or platform account identifiers through the offline core gameplay of Fire and Ashes.

Fire and Ashes does not include advertising networks or analytics SDKs in the game build.

Microphone Use

If microphone-based gameplay features are enabled, microphone input is used locally for real-time gameplay reactions. ASDstudio does not intentionally record, store, or transmit microphone recordings from Fire and Ashes.

Information Collected If You Contact ASDstudio

If you contact ASDstudio for support, privacy, or data protection inquiries, ASDstudio may receive the information you choose to provide, such as your email address, message content, crash description, screenshots, attachments, device details, platform account name, or other information needed to answer your request.

Platform and Store Services

Depending on where you access Fire and Ashes, supported platform or store services may be used for purchases, refunds, entitlements, achievements, crash telemetry, parental controls, platform account features, and store-related functionality.

For information about privacy practices for Epic Games accounts and Epic Online Services, please review the Epic Games Privacy Policy: https://legal.epicgames.com/epicgames/privacy-policy. Other platform providers may provide their own privacy notices and account privacy tools.

How Information Is Captured

ASDstudio may receive information only when you voluntarily contact ASDstudio by email or another support channel. Platform or store-related information may be handled when you buy the game, launch the game through a platform account, unlock platform achievements, encounter a crash, use parental controls, request a refund, or use other platform features.

How Information Is Used

ASDstudio may use information you provide directly for the following purposes:

  • to respond to support, privacy, or data protection requests;
  • to diagnose crashes, compatibility issues, or gameplay problems that you report;
  • to maintain business records related to support and compliance;
  • to comply with legal obligations and platform requirements.

ASDstudio does not use personal data from Fire and Ashes for advertising or sale to third parties.

How Information Is Shared

ASDstudio does not sell personal data. ASDstudio may share information only when necessary for the purposes described in this policy, including with:

  • store or platform providers for purchases, refunds, entitlements, achievements, crash telemetry, parental controls, and platform compliance;
  • service providers that help with support, diagnostics, security, hosting, or compliance;
  • law enforcement, regulators, courts, or other parties when required by law or necessary to protect rights, safety, users, or the integrity of the game.

Data Retention

ASDstudio keeps support, privacy, and data protection correspondence only as long as reasonably necessary to respond to the request, maintain business records, resolve disputes, enforce terms, or comply with legal obligations.

ASDstudio does not intentionally store microphone recordings, analytics profiles, advertising identifiers, or persistent gameplay tracking records for Fire and Ashes.

For platform account data, purchase data, entitlements, achievements, crash telemetry, parental controls, or store-related data handled through a platform account or store account, please use the privacy notice and account tools provided by that platform or store provider.

Payments and Store Purchases

Purchases, refunds, entitlements, tax handling, parental controls, and payment processing are handled by the store or platform where you access Fire and Ashes. ASDstudio does not receive your full payment card number.

Children's Privacy

Fire and Ashes is intended for mature audiences. ASDstudio does not knowingly collect personal data from children through the game. Platform-level age gates, child account protections, and parental controls may be handled by the relevant platform provider.

Your Privacy Choices

You may contact ASDstudio to ask privacy questions, make data protection inquiries, or request access, correction, or deletion of information you directly provided to ASDstudio.

For platform account data, store purchases, parental controls, or data processed by a platform provider, please use the privacy tools and support channels provided by the relevant platform.
